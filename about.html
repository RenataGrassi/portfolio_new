A fish in the sea...

I am an oceanographer, a programmer, and a freelancer who splits the time between life at the sea and life on land, building websites, blogs, and applications. I started programming with R! when I was still a young aspiring oceanographer, in 2007. Then, I knew Matlab in 2013 thanks once again to oceanography and the huge amount of data that we need to process and analyze. I continued my coding journey and started learning Python in 2014. My curiosity and willingness to explore the fascinating and limitless world of technology drove me to other languages and tools like Ruby on Rails, Javascript, CSS, HTML, SQL, and what may come with it (time management, be self-taught, organized, and self-motivated).

I especially enjoy assisting people with the development of their project websites by giving life and color to their ideas and launching their work onto the world wide web. Please feel welcome to contact me if you want to talk about a project you would like to publish online. Let’s make it happen!

In the section 'Portfolio' and 'Side Projects' you can find some projects which I have been working on.
