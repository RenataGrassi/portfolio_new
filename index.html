This Hackathon was organized by Provi in January 2021 and focused on boosting women´s careers. The purpose of the Hackathon was to create a product that would help junior professionals land their first job or find a job that best fits their career goals. My colleagues and I created HackaWork in 3 intense days. It is a platform that connects companies and professionals by hosting technology challenges. HackaWork makes the process easier for companies to find their ideal professional candidate and expands the chances of a junior professional to be seen by potential future employers. Out of the 25 participating teams HackaWork won the 1st prize . You can check the code by clicking on the image to the left.
Team members: Renata Grassi, Camila Cadete, Camila Araujo, Clarice Aoto, Fracielle Abreu e Amanda Bento.

Me and my colleagues created a website for the Le Wagon web development Bootcamp in September 2020. The website is dedicated to tourism services in the city of Arraial do Cabo, Brazil. On one hand, tourists can find offers for tours, accommodation, scuba diving, and other services. On the other hand, entrepreneurs can make offers, but also use the website as a management tool for their company. They can access a dashboard with their sales information and budget statistics, and have tools, such as automatic emailing and generation of PDF documents, that facilitates their daily routine. Note: This site is a prototype and the information contained herein is only demonstrative.
